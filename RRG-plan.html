Initiative 2

Listings Within 6 Miles of Home — The Expired Engine

Two-track Mackin 7-letter sequence, no cold calls, Al drives the door-drops

The single highest-ROI source for Sienna-area listings is expired listings. Two pools, worked differently:

Why no calls
Per Glover U research: door-drop / in-person delivery converts 3–5× higher than email or phone. Al delivering personally to expired sellers is the highest-conversion touchpoint in this entire plan. Cold calls and bulk texts add risk without adding lift.

9 touches per expired over 50 days. Zero phone calls. Every letter carries a QR code routing to a destination matched to the letter's theme (Property Value Range Report request, diagnostic checklist, marketability audit booking, etc.). Letters 4, 5, and 7 carry Make-Offer-For-Immediate-Response offers — "waive pre-listing photography fee" / "free marketability audit by [date]" / "include staging consultation."

Why 7 letters, not 1 or 2
50% of sales happen after the 5th attempt. 92% of agents quit before they get there. Most expired campaigns send one letter and stop. This one sends seven, with a clear next-step on every single touch.

Workload Split

Al works both pools, 50/50 weekly. Fresh expireds for short-term velocity (faster decisions). Old expireds for long-tail listing pipeline (zero competition).

Initiative 3

Monthly Past-Client Email Requesting Referrals

12 emails/year, six-section format, rotating monthly ask

Foundation Work — Inside the Pilot

Command Database Cleanup (Week 1 of pilot). Right now contacts are tagged "CLIENT" without distinguishing closed past clients from active deals from people who initially engaged but ghosted. Cleaning that up unlocks the monthly past-client email, the segmentation tagging, and segment-specific communication paths so each group gets the right cadence.

The Cleanup Process

  1. Audit & triage — pull every contact tagged "CLIENT" and adjacent. Determine actual status (closed past client / active deal / cold lead / pure SOI).
  2. Apply 2-D tagging — Status (Closed Past Client / Active Lead / Cold Lead / Database / DNC) + Tier (A Allstars / B Business / C Coachable / D Database).
  3. Build segment-specific sequences — Closed Past Clients get the monthly email. Cold Leads get a re-engagement sequence. Active Leads stay on current cadence.
